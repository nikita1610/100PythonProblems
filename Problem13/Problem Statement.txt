Problem statement:
Odd and Even Sorting

Input:  [4,2,5,7]

Output:
[4,5,2,7]
or any other possible answer

Explanation:
Given a list of non-negative integers and half of them are even and half of them are odd.
Sort the list such whenever a[i] is odd , i is also odd and whenever a[i] is even i is also even.
You may return any answer that satisifies the condition.
