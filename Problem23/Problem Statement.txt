Problem statement:
Number of subarrays with even sum

Input:  
[1, 2, 2, 3, 4, 1]


Output:
9

Explanation : 
The array {1, 2, 2, 3, 4, 1} 
has 9 such possible subarrays. These are-
 {1, 2, 2, 3}          Sum = 8
 {1, 2, 2, 3, 4}       Sum = 12
 {2}                   Sum = 2 (At index 1)
 {2, 2}                Sum = 4
 {2, 2, 3, 4, 1}       Sum = 12
 {2}                   Sum = 2 (At index 2)
 {2, 3, 4, 1}          Sum = 10
 {3, 4, 1}             Sum = 8
 {4}                   Sum = 4
