Problem statement:
Rotate a matrix by 90 degrees anticlockwise


Input:
n: number of rows and columns=3
matrix= 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9

Output:
3 6 9 2 5 8 1 4 7

Explanation:
Matrix is as below:
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9

Rotating it by 90 degrees in anticlockwise directions will result as below matrix:
3 6 9
2 5 8
1 4 7
