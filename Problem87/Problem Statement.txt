Problem statement:
Minimum moves to equal list elements

Input:
[1,2,3]


Output:
3


Explanation:
Find the minimum number of moves required to make all list elements equal.
A move is defined incrementing list elements by 1 and in 1 move you can modify two elements of list.

[1,2,3]->[2,3,3]->[3,4,3]->[4,4,4]
